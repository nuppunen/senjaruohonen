Senja Ruohonen
senja.ruohonen@metropolia.fi

Senja Ruohonen (born 1992) is a Finnish film student. She is planning to specialize on movie lighting, because she has fallen in love with the atmosphere on movie sets, and she enjoys the cooperative nature of the work. Also, hard and intense work suits her lively temper.

The first time Senja got involved on professional movie making, was while being a trainee on a Finnish movie lighting equipment rental house Valofirma in 2011. There she learned the basics of the industry, and it led her to assist gaffers on several short films, commercials, etc. Senja considers that the most important talents of an assistant are to be quick, brisk and above all alert.

On her spare time, she practices wall climbing to stay in shape, and for the fun of it.
