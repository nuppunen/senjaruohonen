Working with lights is my passion. I want so see as much as possible and I'm constantly seeking for opportunities to travel and learn.

The first time I got involved on professional movie making, was while being a trainee on a Finnish movie lighting equipment rental house Valofirma in 2011. There I learned the basics of the industry, and it led me to assist gaffers on several short films, commercials, etc. After that I've been working on varied productions in Finland and abroad.
