Senja Ruohonen
senja.ruohonen@metropolia.fi

Senja Ruohonen is a young, yet passionate filmmaker who has a promising career as a lighting technician or a gaffer. Currently she is on a second grade on Helsinki Metropolia University of Applied Sciences studying cinematography and editing. It was on the age of 19 when she got involved on a professional film production for the first time. Now, only few years later, she has already been an assistant for some of the best gaffers in Finland on several commercials, short films and on one full length fiction. The next step is to gain some international experience.
